The Clinic

Loosebody London is a professional Massage Clinic in the heart of London, specialising in Sports and Remedial Massage.

We are committed to relieve you of pain and uncomfortable tensions, improving your health and supporting your mental wellbeing. Our treatments are targeted and effective, always based on a careful consultation and tailored to the individual needs and conditions of the client.

Our therapists are University trained and qualified professional Sports and Remedial Massage Therapists certified by ITEC.

We are members of the Federation of Holistic Therapists, complying with the rules and standards of the association and best practice.
